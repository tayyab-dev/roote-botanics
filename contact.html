Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use the hair oil?

For best results, we recommend using our Natural Herbal Hair Oil 2-3 times per week. You can use it as a pre-wash treatment for deep conditioning, or apply a small amount as a finishing oil for daily shine.

Is the oil suitable for all hair types?

Yes! Our formula works beautifully on all hair types—from fine and thin to thick and coarse, straight to curly. The lightweight, non-greasy formula absorbs well without weighing hair down.

How long until I see results?

Most customers notice improved shine and softness within 1-2 weeks. For hair growth and strengthening benefits, consistent use for 4-8 weeks typically shows visible results.
