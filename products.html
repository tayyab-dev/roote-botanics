How to Use

1

Warm the Oil

Pour a small amount (about a tablespoon) into your palm or a small bowl. Gently warm it by rubbing between your palms or placing the bowl in warm water.

2

Apply to Scalp

Part your hair into sections and apply the oil directly to your scalp. Use your fingertips to gently massage in circular motions for 5-10 minutes.

3

Work Through Hair

Apply any remaining oil to the length of your hair, paying special attention to dry ends.

4

Let it Absorb

Leave the oil on for at least 1 hour, or overnight for deep conditioning. Cover with a shower cap if desired.

5

Wash & Enjoy

Wash with a gentle shampoo. You may need to shampoo twice to remove all the oil. Enjoy your soft, nourished hair!

Recommended: Use 2-3 times per week for best results. Consistent use for 4-8 weeks will show visible improvements in hair health and growth.
